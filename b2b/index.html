About KingSauna

Craftsmanship Born From a Passion for Sauna Culture

For over 15 years, KingSauna has been manufacturing premium outdoor saunas and hot tubs from our production facility in Lithuania. What started as a passion for traditional sauna culture has grown into one of the region's leading manufacturers.

We combine time-honoured woodworking traditions with modern engineering — every unit is built to order, with meticulous attention to materials, insulation, and finish. Our saunas aren't mass-produced; they're crafted.
